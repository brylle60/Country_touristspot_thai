Thailand

Thailand offers a diverse range of tourist spots, from vibrant cities like Bangkok and Chiang Mai to beautiful islands like Phuket and Koh Samui. Other popular destinations include the historic Ayutthaya, the natural beauty of Khao Yai National Park, and the unique Phi Phi Islands.
